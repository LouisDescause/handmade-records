Jacob Wright is a 23-year-old indie pop artist who blends heartfelt introspection with timeless melodies. Originally from the Arizona deserts and now based in Los Angeles, his music draws influence from Maggie Rogers, Ryan Beatty, and Harry Styles.

His songwriting explores faith, identity, and transformation — shaped by his time within, and eventual departure from, the Mormon church. His debut album *Face Paint* is an 8-track journey through vulnerability and self-discovery.

Signed to Handmade Records, Jacob represents the kind of artist who turns deeply personal experience into universally resonant music.
