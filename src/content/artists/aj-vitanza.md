Emerging from Buffalo, NY, AJ Vitanza is a pop artist whose storytelling and emotionally resonant songwriting are rapidly gaining traction. Now based in Los Angeles, his music blends alt-pop sensibility with raw emotional honesty — pairing memorable melodies with lyrics that feel like pages from a diary.

His debut EP *PLASTIC HEART* is a 9-track exploration of love, loss, and growing up, showcasing the depth and vulnerability that define his sound.

As a core member of the Handmade Records roster, AJ represents the label's commitment to artists who prioritize authenticity above all else.
