Thumber is a duo from the Bay Area — **Eli Torgersen** and **Virgil Thunes**. Together, they fuse indie songwriting with warm, textured production to create music that lives somewhere between the dancefloor and the bedroom. Their tracks are atmospheric, rhythmic, and impossible to ignore.

Their debut album *Goodbye Car* dropped in December 2025 — an 8-track collection that captures the feeling of driving away from everything you know, windows down, no destination.

Signed to Handmade Records, Thumber represents a new wave of artists redefining what independent music sounds like.
