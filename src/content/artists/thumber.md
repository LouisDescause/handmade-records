Thumber fuses indie songwriting with warm, textured production to create music that lives somewhere between the dancefloor and the bedroom. His tracks are atmospheric, rhythmic, and impossible to ignore.

His debut album *Goodbye Car* dropped in December 2025 — an 8-track collection that captures the feeling of driving away from everything you know, windows down, no destination.

Signed to Handmade Records, Thumber is part of a new wave of LA artists redefining what independent music sounds like.
